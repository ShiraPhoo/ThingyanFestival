History of Myanmar
Thingyan Festival

The history of the Thingyan water festival in Myanmar dating back from a very long time ago originated from the Buddhist version of a Hindu myth.

Nowadays, the Thingyan water festival in Myanmar is renowned as the most cherished event for people to wish for good health and luck to all people who attend.
